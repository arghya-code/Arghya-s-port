🎬 SECTION 1: ENTRY SCREEN — THREE-PANEL 3D GATEWAY
====================================================================

THIS IS THE MOST CRITICAL COMPONENT. Build it with maximum craft.

LAYOUT: Full viewport, three equal panels side by side (33.33% each)
No navbar. No scroll. Pure gateway.

INITIAL STATE (equal 3-way split):
  [  BUILDER  |  OBSERVER  |  NARRATOR  ]
    33.33%        33.33%       33.33%

ON HOVER of any panel:
  Hovered panel expands to 50%, others shrink to 25% each
  Framer Motion: animate={{ flex: isHovered ? 2 : 0.75 }}
  Spring transition: { type: "spring", stiffness: 180, damping: 28 }

ON CLICK of any panel:
  Clicked panel animates to flex: 1 (100% width)
  Others animate to flex: 0, opacity: 0
  After 700ms transition completes → setWorld() 
  Full-screen color flash matching the world's accent color
  Then PageTransition fades in the chosen world

DIVIDER LINES between panels:
  Left divider: gradient — top orange, bottom blue (Builder meets Observer)
  Right divider: gradient — top gold, bottom amber (Observer meets Narrator)
  Each 2px wide with glow animation (box-shadow pulse)
  Small icon at center of each divider: ⚡ left, 📷 right

TOP CENTER (above all panels):
  3D floating text: "ARGHYADIP CHATTERJEE" rendered in Three.js
  Use Text3D from @react-three/drei with a metallic material
  Text slowly rotates on Y axis, glows
